The Five Figures

Motion Graphic Video

{Illustration}

{Video Editing}

The notes I've taken are the essential materials to quickly recall the features of the objects I've learned in my Chinese art history class. In this project, I convert my text notes into motion graphics. While editing the layers, The movement of the patterns highlights the art pieces' characteristics. I have selected 5 significant objects from the 5 different time periods (Neolithic/ Shang/ Han/ Tang/ Song)to illustrate and animate.
