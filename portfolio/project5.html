Speculative Design for
Visually Impaired people

UIUX Design

{User Interface}

{User experience}

Visually impaired people are experiencing many difficulties in their daily life. Since they might have lost sight, an essential method to detect danger. To reduce the chance of injury, we need to be aware of pedestrians, obstacles, traffic lights, etc., while walking on the street. This app aims to point the direction more intuitively by showing road conditions, eliminating interference information, and accepting traffic/warning signals. This software can simplify the visual information received by users, allowing them to receive road conditions more intuitively. A part of the concept is inspired by Tesla autopilot and the Brain-Machine Interface technology.
