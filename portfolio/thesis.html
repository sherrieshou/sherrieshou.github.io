Thesis Project: Pain Index

2022 / Fall
Category: Web Design
Prints / Editorial Exploration

Pain Index：
Pain is an important signal transmitted by the human body to the brain. Pain is the body's call for help. However, many cases around us show that pain can hardly be accurately conveyed (described) in words. As a result, language sometimes hinders empathy between people and makes it more difficult for doctors to diagnose patients.
Phenomenon:
With people's self-restraint, contemporary society suppresses the needs of human limbs and criticizes the value of "fragile."
Some people are unable to express their real needs—self-disciplined, independent individuals.
About mandatory ignoring pain leading to missed treatment/diagnosis (pain medication misuse)

Research Key Words:
Painology, Pain Medicine, Diagnostics, History of Medical Development, History of Disease, mood communication
